To begin, I picked a a small toy and placed it in a standing position on the mat.

After that, I began to sacn the toy. And this is how it came out.

I did not like the outcome of my first scan at all. I felt as though part of the reason that the scan did not turn out so good was because it fell over, even after I used tape to try to secure it. I then decided that I wanted to try again. But this time, I changed the toy into a sitting position so it would remain more balanced than before. I still used tape though, just in case ;)

Changing the position really helped, as I was very satisfied with the new outcome.
